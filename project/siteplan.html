Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a seamless and efficient online platform for customers to rent vehicles. The site will offer a comprehensive and easy-to-use interface for users to browse, select, and reserve a rental car from a diverse fleet of vehicles, including economy cars, sedans, SUVs, and vans.

Scenarios

The audience for this website includes family trips, business trips and adventures. The site is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, with clear and concise information on each vehicle and its features.
